A Rant about Doctor Who- Specifically the Faults with the Chibnall Run Era






The Arts Department

There is nothing short of actual artistry in the last 3 seasons of our beloved sci-fi series. From the effects, cinematography, and costume design- there are are a lot of fantastic visual elements. What lacks however is good design in some of this art.

For example: the main console design, the main setpiece for the series is calustrophonic, poorly lit, and lacks memorial pieces. The promotional material while discussing it, seemed to be not fully thruthful as its rarely shown onscreen.

Ryan as an auxilary character to Graham, bringsstory moments to the table, but was far less interesting. And lastly, poor Yaz who was unrelated to either Ryan orr graham with any real footing, had much more charismatic character, but nothing of note.

It only made sense that with a female lead, and another female companion, that there would be online shipping in Fandom. Overall it is beacause we've barrely had time to know the characters does thasmine feel like it was shoehorned in for diversity.

is that what Doctor Who needed however? Did we need more unnnecesary backstory, large revelations. Porbably not, but was Chibnall doing anything that wasn't say as groundbreaking as creating the Time War, and basing the whole character around it. Or recasting the actor into a completely looking man, writing lore around it just so the series kep going? Probably also yes.
