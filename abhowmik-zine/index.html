BOOKS I READ THIS YEAR

A Certain Hunger by Chelsea G. Summers

An acclaimed food critic decides eating her boyfriends will be fun. The writing is a little pretentious. I give it a 4/5 I support womens' rights and wrongs.

Boy Parts by Eliza Clark

The narrator is the worst person you will ever meet. I give it a 2/5 you would like if you enjoy female manipulators and edgy diet culture.

Comfort Me with Apples by Catherynne M. Valente

A "horror" retelling of Adam and Eve. 3/5 I like how the author wrote this but it did exactly what I expected it to.

The Priory of the Orange Tree by Samantha Shannon

It isn't a genre I thought I would enjoy but I loved the characters and the plot. 5/5 I would read more books in this universe.
