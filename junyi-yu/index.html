Phantom Cube

In ancient mythology there is a magic cube that everyone is looking for. It can change it at will, because every time the holder changes the block and restores it to the original state, the wish will be fulfilled.

But many people died before they got him, because he was a relic of a certain royal authority, and when the royal authority passed away, he was used as a funeral object, and the tomb had heavy organs and unexpected rooms. The only thing known is that the closer you get to the coffin of the kingship, the slower it gets. Even the surrounding water droplets were frozen in mid-air.

After people's word, there was a lucky person who came across it. It's a pity that Phantom Cube did not fulfill his wish like the legend, because the cube felt that the person was not qualified. Instead, he devoured his soul and kicked him out of the tomb. The man went insane permanently until he died, and no one got any news from him.
