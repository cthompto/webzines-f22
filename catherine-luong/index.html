Masquerade

Hey, guys! This is an old dead project I had, which I might as well show off, since it's not going anywhere. It was a big passion project that ended simply because I realized that I didn't listen to any feedback and I paid for it. Basically a bad case of trying to strive for perfection and not cutting your losses when you needed to. It had a whole year's worth of work put into it, so it was too late to fix it and I never really got the motivation to go back to it again.

Humans do not go into the Otherworld, land of the spirits, on a whim. They either come on their own accord, or they were taken by force.

Elise, or the Wanderer, has no idea how she got here, except she has one goal and that is to defeat the Devouring One. The spirit has been pulling humans into the Otherworld and feasting on them and somehow this is Eliseâ€™s job to stop that. Confused and annoyed, she reluctantly accepts her task and sets off to gather as much power as she can. But questions plague her mind. Why is it her, of all people, who had to find a way to help all of these people? Who was she? And most importantly, will she be the same person as she was, before she had lost her memories?

In a battle between being noble or pragmatic, Elise must find all the pieces of her mask to recover her memories and her powers to find out the truth behind this masquerade. Will Elise save everyone or lose herself to the Otherworld?
