Origami - My Hobby

Phoenix

This is Phoenix (designed by Satoshi Kamiya) folded from a 60x60 cm paper. A symbol of power, immortality, and rebirth.
[P.S It was the most complex model I have ever folded.]

Totoro

Growing up watching Ghibli Studio animation, their art and story have had a profound influence on my life and worldview. Thus, I decided to fold Totoro & the Umbrella Leaf to show my love for the film.

  • Totoro designed by Shuki Kato
  • Umbrella Leaf designed by me

Son Goku

To celebrate Goku’s birthday (4/16), I designed and folded his iconic spiky anime hairstyle when he’s in super saiyan form.
[Goku is a fictional character from Dragon Ball series]
